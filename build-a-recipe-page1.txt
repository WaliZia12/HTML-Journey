      <h1> Oreo Milkshake </h1>
      <p> today we will make an oreo milkshake </p>

      <h2> Ingredients </h2>
      <ul>
        <li> oreo biscuits </li>
        <li> milk </li>
        </ul>

        <h2>  Instructions </h2>
        <ol>
          <li> take the blender and add oreo biscuits in it (3 or 4) </li>
          <li> after adding biscuits, add a 2 glasses of milk </li>
          <li> once added, turn on the blender and start mixing </li>
          <li> mix it for 4 to 5 minutes </li>
